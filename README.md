# 📊 Amazon Product Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a Power BI dashboard built to analyze Amazon product data and uncover actionable business insights related to customer satisfaction, discounts, product demand, and value for money.

The objective of the project is to transform raw product and review data into meaningful business intelligence that can support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- Which products drive the most customer demand? 
- Which products are underperforming despite high visibility? 
- Which categories contribute the largest share of products? 
- Which categories have the highest customer satisfaction? 
- Which categories have the lowest customer satisfaction? 
- Are discounts actually associated with better customer ratings? 
- Which products offer the best value for money? 
- Which products are potential risks? 
- What are customers talking about most? 
- What is the overall health of our product portfolio? 

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Summary
Provides a high-level overview of the product catalog, including:
- Total Products
- Total Categories
- Average Rating
- Average Discount
- Total Reviews
- Top Product
- Top Category

---

### 2. Product Performance Analysis

Key analyses include:
- Top Demanded Products
- Underperforming Products
- Best Value for Money Products

---

### 3. Category Analysis

Provides insights into:
- Product Share by Category
- Highest Performing Categories
- Lowest Performing Categories

---

### 4. Customer Insights

Analyzes:
- Relationship between Discount Percentage and Product Performance
- Customer Voice (Review Word Cloud)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Products maintain an average customer rating of **4.09 / 5**, indicating generally positive customer satisfaction.
- The average discount across products is approximately **47%**, reflecting aggressive pricing strategies.
- Electronics-related categories dominate both product availability and customer engagement.
- Higher discounts do not consistently result in higher customer satisfaction.
- Only a small number of products provide exceptional value for money, making them ideal candidates for promotion.
